What We Do

Helping Community Based Organisations To Become Self Sustainable

Built on Smile Foundation’s 20 years of extensive experience working with the grassroots in India, Change The Game India is an India focused offshoot of an international alliance that aims to strengthen the capabilities of social changemakers and grassroots organizations towards better governance and self-sustainability. The program enables them to gain credibility and support. It also helps them become self-sufficient by honing their skills in identifying, accessing and mobilizing locally. available resources.
